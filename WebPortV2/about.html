My Story

I've always sought out opportunities and challenges that are meaningful to me. Although my professional path has has many bumps in the road I never stopped engaging my passion to help others and solve problems (especially the ones nobody else would do) with an open mind.

You never realize how hard it is to talk about yourself until you have to write an about me section haha. I searched far and wide for a single word to describe me as a person and I found a word. The word is savant, the dictionary definition says “ given to inquiry, research, or asking questions; eager for knowledge; intellectually curious”. Personally I couldn't think of a better word to describe the never ending thirst for knowledge I have. That's why I decided to make a career change into a field where the knowledge is never-ending due to the constant technological changes.

The beauty of art

For me art has always been a source of inspiration. Art expands my reality, my way of seeing things. It has a language all its own. Art helps you to see and perceive the world around you in a different way. Art has this way of evoking raw emotion. It can make you happy, or cry; It can draw you in, or repel you; It can soothe your soul or even make you angry. It transcends language, borders and the ages. It provides us with imaginative links to parts of ourselves which might otherwise be inaccessible.
